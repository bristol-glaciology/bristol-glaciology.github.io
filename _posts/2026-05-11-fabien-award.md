Fabien Maussion was this week [awarded the International Glaciological Society’s ‘Firn Award’](https://www.igsoc.org/congrats-to-igs-2025-awardees). This award is for significant scientific and community contributions to glaciology by a mid-career scientist.

Fabien’s [award citatation](https://www.igsoc.org/about/awards/firn-award/fabien-maussion-2025) highlights his amazing contributions to open source science, and also for supporting the development of early career scientists and making glaciology a more inclusive space. This is evidenced by his former PhD student [Lili receiving the Early Career Award](https://www.igsoc.org/about/awards/early-career-scientist-award/lilian-schuster-2025) in the same round.

Congratulations Fabien!
