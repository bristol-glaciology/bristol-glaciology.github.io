## OGGM

Open Global Glacier Model - a modular, open-source model for glacier dynamics.

[Visit OGGM →](http://oggm.org){: .btn .btn--primary}

## pDEMtools

Python tools for downloading and analysing ArcticDEM and REMA digital elevation models.

[Visit pDEMtools →](http://pdemtools.rtfd.io){: .btn .btn--primary}


## FieldApp

iPhone app for to simplify the collection, organisation, and export of datasets whilst in the field.

[Download from the Apple App Store →](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fieldapp-notebook/id6753550214){: .btn .btn--primary}
